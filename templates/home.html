Welcome to Health Management System

Our Health Management System is designed to provide comprehensive health-related information and services.

It includes the following main pages:

  • Disease Prediction from Symptoms: This page allows users to input their symptoms and receive predictions about possible diseases.
  • Medicines Information from Disease: Here, users can search for information about medicines based on the disease they are interested in.
  • Doctors and Hospitals Page from Disease: This page provides details about doctors and hospitals specialized in treating specific diseases.
